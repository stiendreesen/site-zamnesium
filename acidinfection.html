biografie

The last band is Acid infection. Acid infection started in 2010 as a heavy metal band but quickly they decided to put their focus on stoner rock. They consist of 4 members: John, Levi , Thomas and Jimmy. They joined Zamnesium records in the winter of 2016. And they will proudly present their first 4 albums at the official zamnesium records label night the 24th of june.
