Our label

Zamnesium records was founded in 2017 by Stien Dreesen,Kenneth Dobbels, Stans Van den broeck and Larissa Goffin. It was started as part of a project and its initial aim was to release a few very limited edition stoner rock Lp's. As time has moved on Zamnesium records nowadays releases stoner rock tracks of many diffrent bands. The prime motive of the label is to blow new life in the stoner rock genre and to give young new bands a chance in the music sector.
Zamnesium is an independent stoner rock record label that specialises in limited edition, high quality vinyl releases. We don’t discriminate or get distracted by sub-genres – if it rocks, it fucking rocks!

Zamnesium records main artists include Beyond evil, Tripping desert, acid infection And a way to hell.
